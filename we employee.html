Employment Support

At Mother Care Charity, we believe in empowering people through employment. We provide job opportunities, training, and skill development programs to help individuals build a sustainable livelihood. Unlike regular jobs that are mainly profit-driven, charity-related employment focuses on service, social impact, and community development.

Skill Development

Charity-based employment plays a vital role in building sustainable communities. Unlike regular jobs that are mainly profit-driven, charity-related employment focuses on service, social impact, and community development.

Job Placements

Charities often create employment opportunities by hiring staff for administration, fundraising, healthcare, education, and social outreach. They also provide skill development and vocational training for underprivileged people, helping them secure stable jobs. Many charities engage volunteers, and outstanding volunteers often transition into full-time paid positions.

Entrepreneurship Support

Employment projects run by charities enable marginalised groups (such as women, differently-abled individuals or rural youth) to earn a livelihood. This reduces poverty and dependency, promoting self-reliance. Charities frequently organise workshops on entrepreneurship, tailoring, handicrafts, computer literacy, and agriculture.

Women Employment

We believe that empowering women through meaningful employment not only changes their lives but also transforms families and communities for generations to come. Women employment is one of the most powerful tools for social change. Many charities work to provide opportunities for women, especially those from disadvantaged backgrounds, to become financially independent and socially empowered.
